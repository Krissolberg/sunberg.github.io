Destinations

Krabi

The province Krabi in the mainland east of Phuket got everything you wish of a vacation to Thailand. You can enjoy the adventurous beaches, beautiful nature and activities such as mountain climbing, snorkeling and boat trips in the spectacular skerries Phra Nang bay.

Bangkok

Street sellers, busy markets and shiny temples is fighting to get the attention in this Asian metropole. A trip to Bangkok gives you vacation memories that will last a lifetime, but make sure that you don’t plan too much beforehand. A visit to Chinatown could easily take a couple hours more than you think, so with too much planned Bangkok could end up being more stressful than you’d want.

Phuket

Phuket is one of the most known and popular destinations in the south of Thailand. This is where travelers from all over the world is visiting to experience a paradise vacation.
